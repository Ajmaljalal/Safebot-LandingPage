1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SafeBot ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our Service. By using SafeBot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We're committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

  • Contact information: So we can reach you when needed

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Helps us optimize the Service for your device
