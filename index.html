Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SafeBot

How does the security assessment work?

SafeBot uses a voice-driven AI assistant to guide you through a series of questions about your home's physical security (doors, windows, locks) and cyber security (WiFi, passwords, devices). Based on your responses, our algorithm generates a comprehensive security score and personalized security plan.

How long does an assessment take?

A typical assessment takes about 15-20 minutes to complete. You can pause and resume at any time.

Is my security information private?

Yes, SafeBot takes your privacy seriously. All your security data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your information.

Can I use SafeBot for multiple properties?

Yes, you can create separate assessments for different properties within the same account, making it ideal for homeowners with multiple properties or property managers.
